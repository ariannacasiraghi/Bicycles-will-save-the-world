Inspired by Swedish activist Greta Thunberg and mass movement Extinction Rebellion, we want to add our voices to a growing chorus: we must take action now to keep our planet habitable. As a reminder that transport alternatives to our most common choices exist, we will cycle over 5000 km in a route that will draw a massive bicycle across 7 countries of western Europe.

With this, we aim to encourage individuals to cycle as much as possible and prompt governments to invest in suitable and safe infrastructure - following the Dutch model. We do sincerely believe that bicycles can take us a long way in our battle to tackle climate change and environmental breakdown!
